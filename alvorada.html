Executive summary

Palm & Peak proposes to acquire, renovate and resell a 3-bedroom villa in Alvorada 4, Arabian Ranches 1, an established low-density Emaar community with deep end-user demand. The villa is acquired at AED 9.0M, upgraded through a AED 2.0M full renovation that reconfigures the layout from three bedrooms to four and adds a 300 sqft permitted extension, and resold at a target of AED 16.0M within a 6 to 12 month window.

The exit is evidenced, not assumed. An identical Alvorada 4 / 3-bedroom villa on the same 8,080 sqft plot sold for AED 8.8M in September 2025 and resold, renovated, at exactly AED 16.0M in April 2026, a registered Dubai Land Department transaction. Twenty Alvorada villa sales cleared in the past twelve months, confirming a liquid, repeatable market.

Investment thesis

  • Proven exit. A same-footprint renovated Alvorada 4 villa printed AED 16.0M three months ago (DLD registered).
  • Liquid market. 20 registered Alvorada villa sales in 12 months; well-priced stock in Arabian Ranches typically absorbs in 4 to 8 weeks.
  • Controlled value-add. Full interior refit plus a modest, permitted 300 sqft extension at Emaar's tier-1 FAR fee.
  • Downside cushion. Unrenovated same-footprint stock trades AED 8.8M to 9.1M, close to our entry.
  • Clean structure. All-equity, no financing or refinancing risk; single asset, single exit.
